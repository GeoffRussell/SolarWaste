### Introduction

This software estimates waste streams and land used under various PV scenarios.

Initial settings correspond with IEA Net Zero by 2050 targets (see below).

Use the sliders to change assumptions.

Keep in mind that not all assumptions are equal, some are definitely better than others!
Read the model assumptions below for details on the initial parameter choices.
Hover over a bar on the chart to see the precise values ... drag an area to expand the view.

**N.B., the "produced" column is non-cumulative.**
